## 1. Macro & Policy News

- **Historic Milestone:** Dow Jones Industrial Average closed above 50,000 for the first time in history, reaching 50,137 after a major rebound from three consecutive days of losses.

- **Big Tech AI Spending Concerns:** Big Tech stocks announced $660 billion in AI spending plans for 2026. Investors are increasingly concerned that capital expenditures are outpacing the earnings potential of new AI technology, triggering recent selloffs.

- **Software Sector Credit Stress:** The software sector selloff is spreading to debt markets, with bond and loan prices for major companies like Salesforce and ServiceNow declining amid concerns about disruptive AI coding tools.

- **Bank of England Policy:** The BoE held interest rates at 3.75% but signaled future rate cuts are likely as inflation pressures ease.

- **Financial Stability Watch:** Commentary emerging about potential systemic risks, with concerns that "the plate tectonics of the global financial system are moving" and traditional safe haven assets may face pressure in a crisis scenario.

- **Crypto Recovery:** Bitcoin bounced back above $70,000 following the equity market rebound.
