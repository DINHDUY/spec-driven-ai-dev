## 1. Macro & Policy News

- **Federal Reserve Liquidity Operations:** The Fed's Standing Repo Facility (SRF) provided a record $74.6 billion loan on December 31, 2025, secured by $31.5 billion in Treasury bonds and $43.1 billion in mortgage-backed securities. Reverse repo facility placements reached approximately $106 billion.

- **Interest Rate Dynamics:** The Secured Overnight Financing Rate (SOFR) reached 3.87% on December 31, above the SRF rate of 3.75%, driving increased repo market activity.

- **Political Tensions:** Reports indicate tensions between the Trump administration and Fed Chair Powell regarding monetary policy direction and the Fed's balance sheet. Markets are monitoring potential policy shifts.

- **ECB Leadership Speculation:** DNB Governor Klaas Knot is being discussed as a potential candidate to succeed Christine Lagarde at the European Central Bank.

- **Labor Market Concerns:** Weekly jobless claims came in at 231,000 (week ending January 31), above estimates of 212,000. U.S. employers announced over 108,000 layoffs in January—the highest level for that month since 2009.
