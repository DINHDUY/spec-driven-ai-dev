## 1. Macro & Policy News

- **Federal Reserve Liquidity Surge:** The Fed's Standing Repo Facility provided a record $74.6 billion cash loan on December 31, 2025, surpassing the previous record of $50.35 billion from October 2025. This reflects tightening year-end liquidity conditions.

- **Repo Market Stress:** The Secured Overnight Financing Rate (SOFR) reached 3.87% on year-end, above the Fed's Standing Repo Facility rate of 3.75%, indicating elevated funding pressures.

- **Reverse Repo Activity:** Money-market funds and other institutions placed $106 billion in the reverse repo facility—one of the highest levels in recent times.

- **Fed Speaking Calendar:** Federal Reserve Governor Stephen I. Miran and Vice Chair for Supervision Michelle W. Bowman scheduled to discuss supervision and regulation topics in early February.

- **Market Volatility:** February 5, 2026 marked the third consecutive day of losses with risk-off sentiment dominating. Dow shed 600 points and Bitcoin plunged.
